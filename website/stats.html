some more information about Muhammad Ali

Muhammad Ali, also known as "The Greatest," had a long and illustrious boxing career, with numerous victories and championships. Here are some of his match stats: Professional Record: Total fights: 61 Wins: 56 (37 by KO) Losses: 5 Notable fights: Cassius Clay (Muhammad Ali) vs. Sonny Liston I, February 25, 1964: In this fight, Ali won the world heavyweight title for the first time by defeating Liston in a seventh-round technical knockout (TKO). Muhammad Ali vs. Joe Frazier I, March 8, 1971: This was the first of three legendary fights between Ali and Frazier. Frazier won the bout by unanimous decision, giving Ali his first professional loss. Muhammad Ali vs. George Foreman, October 30, 1974: In what was dubbed "The Rumble in the Jungle," Ali defeated Foreman in an eighth-round knockout to regain the heavyweight title. Muhammad Ali vs. Joe Frazier III, October 1, 1975: In their third and final fight, Ali defeated Frazier in a 14-round decision, cementing his status as one of the greatest boxers of all time. It's worth noting that in addition to his boxing career, Ali was also a civil rights activist and cultural icon, known for his charismatic personality and outspokenness.
