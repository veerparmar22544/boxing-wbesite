About Muhammad Ali

Cassius Marcellus Clay Jr., better known as Muhammad Ali, was an American professional boxer and cultural icon. He is widely regarded as one of the most significant and celebrated sports figures of the 20th century, and is known for his outspokenness and charisma both inside and outside of the ring. Ali won numerous titles and awards throughout his career, and is remembered as a symbol of courage, strength, and perseverance.

Selected Matches

Ali vs. Liston II - Muhammad Ali's first heavyweight title defense against Sonny Liston in 1965.

Ali vs. Frazier III - The "Thrilla in Manila" fight against Joe Frazier in 1975, widely regarded as one of the greatest boxing matches of all time.
